Cook with me

I enjoy making everyday foods more fun! All of these recipes are nutritious and full of satisfyingly healthy ingredients, for the health-conscious eater.

  • Steel-Cut Oats

    fruity, hearty, and filling

    serves 1-2 people

    Ingredients:
    -1/2 cup Bob's Red Mill Quick-Cooking Steel-Cut Oats
    -1 cup water, milk, or milk subsitute
    -dollop of nut butter
    -1 tablespoon chia seeds
    - fresh or dried fruit, to taste
    honey or other sweetener, to taste

  • Veggie Omelet

    Cheesy, light, and fluffy

    serves 1 person

    Ingredients:
    -2 eggs
    -salt and black pepper, to taste
    -1 tablespoon milk or water
    -chopped vegetables, like brocolli, onions, etc
    -1/3 cup shredded cheese

  • Turkey Sandwich

    Crispy, tart, and lean

    serves 1 person

    Ingredients:
    -2 slices whole grain bread of choice
    -1/2 apple, sliced thinly
    -cheese, as desired
    -2 tablespoons hummus or mayonnaise
    -10 leaves spinach, washed
    -2 slices turkey breast

  • Baked Chicken Breast

    Sweet, moist, and zesty

    serves 1-2 people

    Ingredients:
    -skinless, boneless chicken breast
    -1 lemon or orange, zested
    -1/2 cup fresh rosemary
    -1/2 cup butter
    -3 cloves garlic
    -any additional seasoning of choice, to taste
    -honey (optional to drizzle)
